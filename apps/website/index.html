How It Works

Three steps to
never type again.

01

Create Your Profile

Enter your details once — name, contact, experience, skills. Everything stays stored locally in your browser's secure storage.

02

Visit Any Job Page

CogniFillz detects fillable fields automatically on LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, and dozens more. Your local AI scores the role against your resume in real time.

03

One Click, Done

Hit Autofill, review the populated fields, tailor your resume if needed, and submit. Your application is tracked automatically.
